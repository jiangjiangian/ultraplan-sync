# Credits — 《尋傘記：政大山下篇》

Third-party assets used in this game. The runtime binaries live under
`resources/` (user-managed, not committed); this file is the shipped
record of where they come from and under what terms.

## Sprites — characters & NPCs

**PIPOYA FREE RPG Character Sprites 32x32** — the player personas and the
five NPC archetypes (西裝學長 / 助教 / 福利社阿姨 / 學霸 / 苦主).

- Author: **Pipoya**
- Source: https://pipoya.itch.io/pipoya-free-rpg-character-sprites-32x32
- License (per pack page): free for commercial and personal use, free to
  edit; may **not** be redistributed or resold as standalone assets.

## Sprites — vending machines

**Pixel Art Vending Machines** — the Ch2 圖書館 and Ch4 集英樓便利商店 stalls.

- Author: **karsiori**
- Source: https://karsiori.itch.io/pixel-art-vending-machines
- License: per the itch.io pack page (free asset pack — used as a game

These are **not licensed assets** — they are third-party IP used as small
decorative fan elements in this non-commercial NCCU coursework build only.
Attribution does not grant a license; they must not be redistributed or
used commercially.

- **ちいかわ (Chiikawa)** — the「始～」bunny, shown as a pulsing statue in the
